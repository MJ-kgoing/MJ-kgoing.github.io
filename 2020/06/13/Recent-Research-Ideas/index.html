Recent Research Ideas

Posted on 2020-06-13

As my summer vocation just started, I plan to use this time to research on some topics that I’m interested in or maybe start up some projects, and the first step to begin is brainstorming and write whatever in my mind down:

The Spread of Worldwide Protests.

Recently, the death of George Floyd has triggered demonstrations and protests in more than 75 U.S. cities and around the world against police brutality, police racism, and lack of police accountability, implying the oppressed emotions of the rasicm sufferers and protestors. I’m interested in this event because I really wonder why would the death of a single man strike such a powerful chord that stimulates so many people to gather together, even during the pandemic. To go further on that quesiton I might need to do more research on Crowd psychology or sociology, or maybe the thing isn’t that complicated.

Anxiety and Emotions Expressing

Since the start the pandemic, my dad has been in an unstable emotions mostly because he started to speculate in the stock market and didn’t get an favorable outcome. He spent already too much time and effort in it and was not willing to ask for professional advices, making himself really anxious and stressed. Therefore, I would like to find a suitable way to ease his stress and persuade him to try more things out in his life as he doesn’t normally do so.

Establishing a Small Society in A Nursing Home.

I recently watched a video about a Japanese nursing house where they release their own curency and establish a series of “society rules” for the old people to earn “money”. They even create small gambling games as it somehow decrease the their chance of getting Alzheimer’s disease. These succefully help the old people to find their value again and make them feel that they are not disconnected with the society. I don’t know if it’s possible to try this in one of the HK nursing home, but I think it would be really interesting and helpful. However, I definitely need to do some field trip and research to determine the practicity of this idea.
