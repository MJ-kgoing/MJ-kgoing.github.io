2020.0202 My First Python Lesson

I just had my first Python online class and I just want to quickly record this as believe the journey of learning this new coding language will be playing a pivotal role in my future learning.

How I felt before class:

I wouldn’t say I’m an experienced code writer nor a beginner. Yes, I do have experience which I gained from the previous digital design/computer science classes in school but as I am not a technology enthusiasm, I didn’t input other time outside of class. Hence, I felt ensure about my ability on entering the course even after the research I did that told me Python is a language with low threshold and accessible, especially when the class has already reached chapter 4 according to the textbook. However, as this is a new experience of learning to me, curiosity and freshness have also been taken to the class.

What we learned in class:

This class covered all the content of the usage lists, including the list data type, the index, getting a list’s length, etc.

How I felt after class:

The first sensation I have is the feeling of proud. I was proud of myself because I found myself being able to catch up with the class, understanding the concept, and outputting them on my laptop. This plus the compliment I received in class gave me motivation to do further exploration on coding. Beside, I seem to sense the fun part of coding and I wish this feeling would increase in the future with the progress of my study.

My current plan and goal is to focus on building the understanding of the foundation of Python by re-taking my missed class of chapter 1-3. Extracting information from the lessons maximumly in order to pave a better road for future learning.
