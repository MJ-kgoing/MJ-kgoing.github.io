Art and Design Club

Posted on 2021-04-13
What is it

This is a club that I created with my friends who are also interested in design and art. In this community we have people with different art specialties such as architechture, blender, fusion 360, painting, procreate, shoe design, etc. We gathered together in order to share our skills to the students in lower grades and do some fun projects with them in a way that can connect people in this big school. The ANDC club is part of the school extracurricular exercise system that other students can sign up for. Currently, we are offering an 1 hour session on Thursday every week in which we teach some basic course and draw/design with students from year 7 to 11, supervised by the school design/art teachers.

What do I do

As a person who watched a lot of anime during primary and middle school, I spent quite a lot of time drawing anime characters and sometimes I create profile picture for others. Therefore, in this club I designed a course called “anime facial drawing” in which I teach some basic techniques of drawing the head of anime characters. Due to covid, we are only able to teach virtually, thus I collaberated with one of my friend who teach procreate so the students can immediately apply what they learn on their iPad.

For each lesson, I watch tons of tutorial on the topic I would teach before, prepare the materials, and planed out the timeline beforehand. During the class I spend the first half teaching and the other giving feedback and such. We also send out feedback forms to the students to improve our course constantly. Due to the time limit, I have to swop with other “teachers” for them to deliver their knowledge.

What do I learn
  • Teaching is hard :(
  • Interaction in a lesson is vital, which is why now I understand teachers always require us to turn on video.
  • In order to attract students’ attention, I can’t be talking all the time without constant check on the students nor delivering the information in a monotonous format。
  • Even though all the preparation is behind the scene, you can still see it in the final work, which is why we can’t have a fluke mind at any time.
